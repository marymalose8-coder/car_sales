# car_sales

The "Vehicle Sales and Market Trends Dataset" provides a comprehensive collection of 
information pertaining to the sales transactions of various vehicles. This dataset encompasses 
details such as the year, make, model, trim, body type, transmission type, VIN (Vehicle 
Identification Number), state of registration, condition rating, odometer reading, exterior and 
interior colors, seller information, Manheim Market Report (MMR) values, selling prices, and 
sale dates.

#Dataset

##Column name discription :

YEAR-The manufacturing year of the car — when it was built.

MAKE-The brand or manufacturer of the vehicle (e.g. BMW, Chevrolet).

MODEL-The specific model of that make (e.g. X3, Cruze).

TRIM-The trim or grade level of the car — this indicates a specific version/variant of a model, with different features/options.

BODY-The body style (or “shape”) of the car: SUV, Sedan, Convertible, etc.

TRANSMISSION-Type of gearbox: automatic or manual in your data.

VIN-Vehicle Identification Number — a unique identifier for each car.

STATE-	The U.S. state in which the car was sold (or where the auction took place). 

CONDITION	-Numeric rating of the car’s condition at the time of sale. (In similar datasets, this is often on a scale, e.g., 1–5, where higher is better.) 


ODOMETER-The odometer reading — how many miles (or kilometers) the car has traveled. 

COLOR-The exterior color of the car.

INTERIOR-The color (or material) of the car’s interior. 

SELLER-The entity (person or company) that sold the car — in your data this seems to include “financial services remarketing (lease)”, “the hertz corporation”, etc. 

MMR-Manheim Market Report value — an estimate of the car’s wholesale market value based on historical auction data. 

SELLINGPRICE-The actual price the car sold for in that transaction / auction. 

SALEDATE-The date (and sometimes time) on which the sale occurred.
